Nairobi, Kenya

Nairobi is the capital city of Kenya. It is sometimes referred to as City Under the Sun. This is the only capital city in the world with a national park where one can view various animals like giraffes, lions, and buffalo. In East Africa, Nairobi is the Silicon Savannah. It is where M-Pesa was born, has hundreds of start-ups, and major technology companies have their offices here.
